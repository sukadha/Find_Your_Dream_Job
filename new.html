List of Best Job Search Sites

Naukri

Naukri, founded in 1997, is a leading job portal in India, attracting millions of visitors monthly. It offers diverse job opportunities, including work-from-home options, and is known for its user-friendly interface.

Cutshort

Cutshort, founded in 2015, uses AI to connect you with the best job search portals in India. It analyzes your skills and career goals to recommend relevant job boards, saving you time and effort. Cutshort acts as a career matchmaker, directing you to platforms with the best opportunities for your profile.

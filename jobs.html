List of Best Job Search Sites

Naukri

Naukri, founded in 1997, is a leading job portal in India, attracting millions of visitors monthly. It offers diverse job opportunities, including work-from-home options, and is known for its user-friendly interface. Many users have successfully found jobs through the platform, making it a reliable choice for job seekers across various fields.

LinkedIn

LinkedIn, founded in 2003, is a top platform for professionals in India to network, showcase skills, and find job opportunities. It offers targeted job searches, direct communication with recruiters, and a professional profile to highlight your expertise. Millions use LinkedIn daily, making it a powerful tool for career growth.

Glassdoor

Glassdoor, founded in 2008, is a valuable resource for Indian job seekers. It offers company reviews from current and former employees, salary information for various roles, and interview tips shared by candidates. This helps you make informed decisions about potential employers and prepare effectively for interviews.

Indeed

Indeed, launched in 2004, is a global job portal popular in India. It offers a vast collection of job listings across various industries, including software companies. The platform's easy search feature, using simple keywords or advanced filters, helps job seekers quickly find opportunities that match their skills and interests.

Upwork

Upwork, started in 2013, is a top platform for Indian freelancers, offering diverse projects in writing, design, coding, and more. It allows remote work, helping you maintain work-life balance. Upwork helps build your portfolio and find long-term clients, making it ideal for those seeking freelance careers.

FoundIt

Foundit, formerly known as Monster, has been a key player in Indian job hunting since 2010. It offers a wide variety of job postings across multiple fields, including IT, marketing, finance, and healthcare. Foundit also features remote work options and resources for fresh graduates, making it a versatile platform for job seekers at all stages of their careers.

Times Jobs

TimesJobs, founded in 2004 and managed by the Times Group, has over 25 million registered job seekers. It offers personalized job recommendations based on your profile, a dedicated section for government jobs, and TechGIG.com for IT jobs. TimesJobs provides a wide range of job postings across various industries, making it a versatile platform for all job seekers in India.

Freshers World

Freshersworld, founded in 2006, is tailored for fresh graduates and those with limited experience. It offers resources and job postings relevant to entry-level roles. The platform provides free basic services, allowing users to create profiles, search for jobs, and apply without any initial cost.

Jobs For Her

JobsForHer, founded in 2015 by Neha Bagaria, is tailored for women job seekers in India. It offers curated job opportunities, flexible work options, and valuable career support like guidance workshops and interview tips. The platform also provides networking opportunities, helping women connect and grow professionally.

Cutshort

Cutshort, founded in 2015, uses AI to connect you with the best job search portals in India. It analyzes your skills and career goals to recommend relevant job boards, saving you time and effort. Cutshort acts as a career matchmaker, directing you to platforms with the best opportunities for your profile.
